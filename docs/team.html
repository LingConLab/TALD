Team

The project was designed by Michael Daniel, Konstantin Filatov, Timur Maisak, George Moroz, Timofey Mukhin, Chiara Naccarato, and Samira Verhees.

The TALD project would not have been realized in its present form without the advice and assistance of a number of people.
